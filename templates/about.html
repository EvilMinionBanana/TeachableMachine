Our History:

It all started in early February, 2024, so not really that long ago. CAS Comp Sci got assigned a project to use Teachable Machine® and make it do... something. One student, Jamie Schimmer, decided to make it so whenever you say "Park" the Parks and Rec® theme song plays! How exciting! Doesn't everyone just love when music starts randomly playing but the music has a meaning to what was just said? YES! We (I) hope you enjoy this totally fun but not at all useful website!

Our Mission:

Make people laugh and/or smile. Wow, that was brief!
